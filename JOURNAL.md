# July 10th
Planning out the case.

Time spent = 1 hr

Recently, I got into playing tekken 8 A LOT which is the reason I wanted to build this controller. Playing on a regular controller feels pretty inaccurate and I end up doing moves I never meant to due to the d-pad since its pivoted rather than being separate buttons for each direction. A leverless fixes this issue since it is only made out of buttons, each for their own action. Obviously this won't guarantee I'll become a god at this game, but having more control will make it easier for sure.

Before I started, working on the layout I watched a video made by 'Mad Mod Labs' which helped significantly as it helped me understand how I really want to build my controller. I looked around the internet to see how I big I want my controller to be, and how many buttons I would like. I ended up settling with 12 buttons, and a smaller size.

I also decided to make this icecream sandwich themed, hence why its called ICS.

For the firmware, I'll be using the GP2040-CE. This firmware is pretty much built for stuff like this and doesn't require coding either, just have to make sure to wire the pins to their correct assignments listed on the website.

I decided not to use any headers, since when I used them for my last project I kinda messed it up and bent everything. I made the hole for the screw a little smaller than the threads of the screw, so that it can carve its own way in, and as for the pico, I added 4 pillars for the pico to slide in, and then melt the pillars to lock it in place. The plate is 3.5 mm thick.

This turned out much more cheaper than expected. I was originally planning to use akko silvers, as mentioned previously, but I forgot that they come in packs of 45 and all I needed were 12. So, I settled on use the HMX Xinhais. These switches come in packs of 10, are also very light as hell so they will do the job, and are cheaper to purchase since I'm only buying 20. I'm also going to handwire everything, so I don't need a pcb either which I assume also saved a decent amount of money.
